Fr. Agnel Multipurpose School

The brainchild of late S R Prashad, was established on August 15th, 2001 with the objective of providing comprehensive education to students up to Higher Secondary Certificate Examination. The school is affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Education.

The foundation stone of the school building was laid on June 24th, 2003 by Shri Prakash Paranjpe, member of parliament, and in the presence of Fr. Bento Rodrigues, Superior Agnel Ashram Fathers.

Motto of our School

Always Excellence is the motto of this School. This principle is impregnated in each and every plan and programme of the School, for therein lies the best device and driving force to foster in every student the qualities of National Integration, uprightness and brotherhood..

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to mould children into Nation builders who will make a living, a life and a difference in our world. We envision that S. R. Group of schools will always remain a Learning, Nurturing Organisation, wherein, the home, school and community will be knit together in a trusting partnership, to ensure personal and academic excellence in all children. We accept every member of the school family, as a unique and integral part of a divine design. To provide a learning environment which empowers students and staff to maximize their personal, creative and academic potential in order to become life long learners and responsible citizens of India and the world.
